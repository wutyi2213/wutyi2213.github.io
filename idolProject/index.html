Biography

Chit Thu Wai (Burmese: ချစ်သုဝေ; born 10 August 1984) is a Burmese actress, singer and medical doctor.She has achieved fame and success as an actress and singer. She is a practicing Roman Catholic.She was interested in singing since childhood, the love of music was seeded in her when her father sent her to a Sunday school.

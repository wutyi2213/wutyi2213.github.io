Early life and Education

Chit Thu Wai was born on 10 August 1984 in Yangon, Myanmar.Her parents are U Zaw Win and Daw De De. Her father U Zaw Win, is a music producer and founder of the Metal Zone Studio. She is the youngest daughter of two siblings, having an elder sister, Chit Su Wai, a singer. She attended high school at Basic Education High School No. 3 Mingalar Taung Nyunt and graduated from University of Medicine 2, Yangon with a degree in M.B.B.S.

Career

Music career:She began her music career in 2000 as a singer in performing at the Thingyan music concerts. The same year, she launched her debut album "Tho...Phay Phay Nae May May" (To...Father and Mother) which was a duet album with her sister Chit Su Wai.

Actor career:She made her acting debut with a leading role in the film Nay Ta Chan La Ta Chan (Half Moon Half Sun) alongside Sai Sai Kham Leng, Ye Lay. The film was directed by Zaw Ko Ko and released in 2006.
